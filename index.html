Hi I'm Charlie,
and I'm a Games Programmer.

As a masters student studying Commercial Games Development at UWE, I am passionate about all aspects of game programming. With a strong focus on creating clean, modular, and extensible code, I am always looking for ways to improve my skills.

My favourite videogame genres include survival-horrors, action-adventure, and first-person shooters.
